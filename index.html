Minionese, the language of Minions!

Minionese, or often referred as Minion Language or Banana Language,the gibberish that the hysterical yellow Minions from the Despicable Me moviesis actually a bunch of words taken from other languages (Conlangs || constructed languages), as well as some onomatopoeia (words that sound like what they mean), or just syllables that sound silly together.
